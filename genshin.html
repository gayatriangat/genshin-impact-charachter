QIQI

A Cryo charachter in Genshin Impact

Profile

An apprentice and herb gatherer at Bubu Pharmacy. "Blessed" by the adepti a with a body that cannot die.

Appearance

Qiqi has light purple hair reaching her shoulders at the sides with a long plait in the back, pale skin, and magenta colored eyes. Her fingernails are black. Qiqi usually wears purple themed clothing and has several talismans stuck on her. Her attire consists of a purple frock and a deep purple beret with a red-purple (magenta) tassel on top and her Cryo Vision embedded on the side. She wears a pair of simple earrings; one black and one white. She also wears black heels and white stockings that are wrapped in mummy-like bandages.

Skills

  • Healer
  • Cryo DMG
  • puts cryo mark on enemy so on hit,Normal and Charged Attacks regenerate HP for your own party members and nearby teammates.

Combat Info

Normal Attack: Perform up to 5 rapid strikes.

Charged Attack: Consumes a certain amount Stamina to unleash 2 rapid sword strikes.

Plunging Attack:Plunges from mid-air to strike the ground below, damaging opponents along the path and dealing AoE DMG upon impact.
